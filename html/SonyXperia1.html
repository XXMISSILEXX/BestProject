The first smartphone with a 21:9 "screen

If smartphone manufacturers are now making devices with 18: 9 or 19: 9 screen ratios, the new flagship with Sony has a screen ratio of up to 21: 9.

Xperia 1 has a 6.5-inch screen that supports Sony's BRAVIA X1 display technology for deeper and more natural blacks.

You may not know, this is the only 4K HDR OLED screen in the world.

According to Sony's share, the display quality on the Xperia 1 is up to the standard for filmmakers to use for their video editing work.

In addition, the device is also IP68 certified for dust and water resistance and is protected by Corning Gorilla Glass 6 on both the front and back.

The first smartphone with 3 Sony cameras

If in the past Sony was often criticized because it made lenses for every smartphone on the market, but every smartphone can beat the product from Sony.

However, with the Sony Xperia 1, this is a makeover of Sony about the camera with the rear camera cluster with 3 sensors.

All 3 rear camera sensors on Xperia 1 have a resolution of 12 MP, including a telephoto lens and an ultra-wide-angle lens.

The new BIONZ X algorithm allows the Xperia 1's Eye AF camera to focus super-accurately, super-fast.

Sony is giving photographers more control on the Xperia 1, with the "Cinema Pro" mode.

Top performance with Snapdragon 855

Providing hardware power for the device is the Qualcomm Snapdragon 855 processor manufactured on the 7 nm process for a clock speed of up to 2.84 GHz.
