Of course, a smartphone with a top-notch design will not lack hardware power, Galaxy Note 20 Ultra uses a powerful 8-core Exynos 990 chip, which takes care of almost every task from surfing the web, watching movies, and so on. Sketching images or editing high-resolution videos.
