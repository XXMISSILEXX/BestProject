Design makeover

With the Xperia XZ2, Sony's once famous "Omni Balance" design language has gone into the past to make way for a new and more groundbreaking design.

The biggest change of the Xperia XZ2 compared to the Xperia XZ1 is that the back has been curved to make it easier for our hands to hold the device.

Gorilla Glass 5 material appears on both sides of the Xperia XZ2 and the fingerprint sensor is lined up in the middle of the back to create an aesthetic balance.

The glass of the Xperia XZ2 is 2.5D curved on all four sides to make the body more seamless.

Sharp screen

The Xperia XZ2 is also the first Sony smartphone to own a 5.7-inch edge-to-edge 18: 9 screen.

It is quite unfortunate that the screen of the Xperia XZ2 only stops at Full HD + but possesses the famous HDR technology from Sony and the ability to convert content from SDR to advanced HDR.

Top performance

Xperia XZ2 will use Snapdragon 845 processor with 4 GB RAM and 64 GB internal memory.

With this processor, there is no doubt that the Xperia XZ2 will be one of the most powerful smartphones at the moment.

The device will run Android 8.0 with a gentle Sony interface that promises to bring an impressive response speed.

Still just a single camera

Xperia XZ2 is equipped with a Motion Eye camera of 19 MP resolution and does not have a dual camera.

A valuable improvement on the XZ2 is that it can record super slow motion movies, up to 960 frames per second at Full HD resolution.

Besides, the selfie camera of the device has a resolution of 5 MP, which sounds unimpressive, but in return, you can use the 3D Creator application to create your 3D face with this selfie camera cluster.

Top sound

This time Xperia XZ2 has been removed by Sony with a 3.5mm headphone jack and according to Sony, this helps the company to deliver better sound quality when listening through headphones.

In addition, the speakerphone of the device is also very impressive with the S-Force Front Surround System technology from Sony, thereby helping the Xperia XZ2 to have 20% louder volume than the Xperia XZ1.
