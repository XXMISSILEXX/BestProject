High-end, trendy edge-to-edge design

Galaxy Tab S7 retains the monolithic, angular metal design like its predecessor Samsung Galaxy Tab S6 before it, feels luxurious and high-end, very comfortable and comfortable in the hand.

The side edges are beveled straight, rounded at the corners, finished with a sturdy metal shell that is less prone to sweat and fingerprints, the finishing ability is extremely high and solid.

The power button and volume up and down buttons are arranged on the left edge, while the 4-speaker system fine-tuned by AKG of the device is placed on the top and bottom edges, for good sound quality, serving the needs of entertainment. mobile mind.

Unlike its brother, the Galaxy Tab S7 is only equipped with a one-touch fingerprint sensor located on the side.

Tab S7 has an overall ultra-thin and light size with side dimensions of 253.8 mm long, 165.3 mm wide, 6.3 mm thick and 500 grams in weight, easy to put in a backpack and carry around.

Sharp display, 120 Hz . refresh rate

Galaxy Tab S7 is equipped with an 11-inch LCD screen with WQXGA resolution (2,560 x 1,600 pixels) with 100% DCI-P3 color gamut coverage and HDR support.

The bezels around the screen have also been thinned, making it easier to see for a spacious use, displaying a lot of information but still ensuring high sharpness.

In particular, Samsung has raised its tablet to a new level when equipping the Galaxy Tab S7 with a 120 Hz refresh rate screen to provide the most eye-catching experience, suitable for gaming, watch Netflix or even work.

In addition, the screen on the Galaxy Tab S7 also supports an S-Pen with low latency like in the Galaxy Note 20 Ultra and a response time of 9 ms to give users the most natural writing experience.

Support smart devices

First, the S Pen on Tab S7 has also been greatly improved, you can create quick actions such as back, home, recent, smart select and screen rotation, better support for multiple actions services such as slideshows.

The S Pen magnetic strip connects to the camera and is flat, not concave like the previous generation, as well as equipped with a keyboard that connects to the Tab S7 via magnets, for use as a laptop.

DeX mode provides a desktop-like interface, and supports a wide range of leading Microsoft software including Word, Excel, Powerpoint, and more. It's a truly all-round Android tablet. the most so far.

Dual camera with quality

The back of the Galaxy Tab S7 is equipped with a dual camera setup consisting of a 13 MP main sensor and a 5 MP ultra-wide lens.

The quality of photos taken on the device is quite good, able to meet your photography needs. In addition, the Galaxy Tab S7 is also capable of recording sharp 4K high-quality video.

The front of the device is equipped with an 8MP selfie camera for taking selfies and video calls with high quality.

Powerful performance, leading the Android tablet segment

Not only impressing the design and screen, the Galaxy Tab S7 also presents itself as an even better device when equipped with the powerful Snapdragon 865+ processor, which is the latest and best chip from Qualcomm. at now.

With the score that the Galaxy Tab S7 measured on Antutu is 581,720 points, it easily helps the device to respond well to tasks from basic to advanced, including VR virtual reality activities or 3D games that require graphics levels. high.

In addition, the Galaxy Tab S7 is also equipped with 6 GB RAM, 128 GB storage memory and a built-in sim tray that can call and text like a smartphone.

Streamlined One UI, smart multitasking features that let you open and use three apps simultaneously on the screen.

Large battery capacity, support super fast charging technology

Galaxy Tab S7 is equipped with a huge 8000 mAh battery, which helps you to use it all day long without worrying about running out of battery while in use.

The device provides about 7 hours and 30 minutes of battery life when browsing the web, 6 hours and 45 minutes when watching videos and nearly 10 hours of gaming.

What's even more impressive is that this tablet also supports 45W fast charging, so users can quickly use the device again in just a short time of charging.
