Familiar design but still exudes luxury beauty

iPhone Xs Max retains the familiar yet modern design like the previous iPhone X model with an overall fit with soft and seamless curves. In addition, the device is covered by a stainless steel frame and a glossy glass back. The phone also has a luxurious gold version besides the two traditional colors of gray and silver.

Sharp screen with eye-catching colors

The 64GB iPhone Xs Max smartphone will give you a great experience with a 6.5-inch wide screen and a resolution of 1242 x 2688 pixels. This is the largest screen ever, but thanks to the borderless design, the overall is not too big, causing stress and discomfort for users. In addition, the image quality is further enhanced by HDR10 technology with a contrast ratio of up to 1 000 000:1.

Powerful configuration, saving power

Powering the iPhone Xs Max 64GB is the A12 Bionic processor manufactured on the 7nm process that enhances the ability to handle all tasks and optimize energy savings. Together with iOS 12 operating system, the device gives users a smooth performance, easy multitasking and your operations also become faster. In addition, the device also gives more than a day of operation thanks to the larger capacity battery and the machine's energy-saving chip.

Camera with attractive parameters and many unique features

The back of the iPhone Xs Max is a 12MP dual camera system equipped with many attractive features. The ability to adjust the aperture from f/1.4 to f/1.6, OIS optical image stabilization technology or Smart HDR feature helps you capture every moment in the most authentic way. Not only that, the device is also integrated with AI artificial intelligence to give users the best photos.

The selfie camera on the front is no less competitive with a 7MP resolution that also supports AI and some unique features. Including the ability to take photos to remove fonts thanks to improvements from the TrueDepth camera system or the interesting Animoji feature that gives you many new emoticons.
