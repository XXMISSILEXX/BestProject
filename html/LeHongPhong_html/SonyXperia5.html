OLED display with 21:9" aspect ratio

After Sony Xperia 1, Sony continues to use the 21: 9 aspect ratio screen for its new Xperia 5.

For those who don't know, the 21:9 aspect ratio screen is higher than the 18:9 screen. Since the screen is taller, it allows users to view more content while surfing the web and social networks.

Not only that, but the screen of the Sony Xperia 5 is also perfect for watching movies as most movies are shot in the 21:9 aspect ratio.

This is an OLED screen so it can display deeper blacks and can reproduce vibrant colors, both of which will make the content displayed on the screen look much more appealing to watch.

The monitor also supports HDR, meaning that if you watch HDR content on it, you can enjoy a wider color gamut, which will further enhance the movie viewing experience...

Snapdragon 855 processor

This Sony smartphone also comes with Qualcomm's flagship processor, Snapdragon 855, the flagship processor found on many high-end smartphones in 2019.

It has four high-performance processor cores, four energy-efficient processor cores and Adreno 640 GPU graphics. Currently, Snapdragon 855 is one of the most powerful mobile processors on the market.

When experiencing games with high configuration, the device can still run smoothly with ease and provide a great user experience.

Besides, With 6 GB RAM + 128 GB ROM, you can safely open many applications at the same time without fear of "lag". The Xperia 5 also allows you to expand the storage space via a microSD card.

High quality camera

Sony Xperia 5 owns 3 rear cameras including: 12 MP main sensor, 12 MP telephoto sensor and 12 MP wide-angle sensor (both with optical image stabilization (OIS) and electronic image stabilization (EIS). .

About the camera app. Xperia 5 offers many impressive features to capture and edit photos (such as manual focus or adjust contrast) instead of using camera AI technology to beautify.

In addition, you can also adjust the aspect ratio as you like, such as 16:9 or 4:3.

Xperia 5 produces beautiful images, with depth and sharp image details. You just need to take pictures, let the camera take care of the rest.

Not only that, the machine also comes with the Cinema Pro application - allowing you to shoot and adjust professional videos.
