Design

On the front, Apple has not changed the design of the iPhone 11 Pro Max (compared to last year's iPhone XS Max). But on the back, everything is much improved: the back is finished from frosted glass, limiting scratches and leaving fingerprints - a marked upgrade compared to its predecessor.

Other details in the design are very similar to what Apple has done in recent years: 'rabbit ears', stainless steel chassis, lightning port located at the back of the device and symmetrical speaker clusters (but only one real speakers).

The really new element in the design is the rear camera cluster. The lens bezel is meticulously machined for very sharp cuts. Therefore, do not put the device in a pocket with another device, iPhone 11 Pro Max can easily scratch another screen.

In particular, the colors of iPhone 11 Pro Max are very attractive, including dark blue, space gray, silver and gold. Dark blue color will very easily make users 'pocket the wallet'.
