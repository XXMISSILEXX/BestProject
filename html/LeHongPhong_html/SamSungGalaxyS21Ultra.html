Breakthrough in trendy design

Not only for communication and entertainment, the Samsung Galaxy S21 Ultra 5G is also a fashion accessory that affirms the owner's position. The appearance is surprisingly slim and slim at only 165.1 x 75.6 x 8.9 mm and weighs 228 g despite having to "carry" a large battery.

The back of the device is covered with a durable, luxurious matte finish that both minimizes fingerprints and creates a sophisticated and surprisingly attractive appearance.

In addition, the Galaxy S21 Ultra 5G is Samsung's first S flagship model to support the S Pen, a tool that is already familiar to many Galaxy Note users. By using Wacom technology, the pen provides a more comfortable and realistic grip.

The device supports water and dust resistance according to IP68 standards. Thanks to that, it can be submerged in water to a depth of 1.5 m for 30 minutes.

More visually satisfying than ever

Samsung Galaxy S21 Ultra 5G owns a large screen size up to 6.8 inches. The "less curved" infinity screen and thin bezel around, and the rather small Infinity-O punch-hole selfie camera help the device achieve the desirable 20: 9 screen ratio.

In particular, the device supports HDR10+ technology and 120 Hz refresh rate for smooth operation, scrolling and the ability to self-adjust frequency to save battery.

Not only protected by Gorilla Glass Victus, the screen of Samsung Galaxy S21 Ultra 5G is also certified for eye protection (Eye care) from SGS to significantly block the harmful effects of blue light, outstanding with its properties. Eye Comfort Shield feature.

Galaxy S21 Ultra 5G is equipped with a fingerprint sensor hidden under the screen for fast and accurate unlocking speed with high security.
