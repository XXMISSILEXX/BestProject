Modern design, fashion

The first impression of the POCO X3 Pro is that the back of the device is finished in a smooth, glossy form, while the two side areas have been roughened to provide a better grip than its predecessor.

Overall, POCO X3 Pro still has a design with an improved plastic outer shell and frame that is quite light but still ensures certainty with the ability to resist sweat or fingerprints, making your phone always as beautiful as new. even without a protective case.

The device comes with 3 color versions: Phantom Black, Metal Bronze and Frost Blue for users to choose according to their own preferences.

Sharp entertainment screen, smooth 120 Hz frequency

The POCO X3 Pro screen has a size of 6.67 inches with Full HD + resolution with an aspect ratio of 20: 9 and a thin bezel that provides a large display space.

Moreover, the screen of POCO X3 Pro also supports a large refresh rate of up to 120 Hz, providing a smooth feeling in touches, swipes as well as switching between applications.

At the same time, the high 240 Hz touch sampling rate reduces touch latency and provides better in-game sensitivity control, taking your gaming experience to the next level.

In particular, the POCO X3 Pro screen is equipped with durable Gorilla Glass 6 during use, which helps the device to survive drops from a height of up to 1.6 m on hard, rough surfaces.

Powerful performance with Snapdragon 860 chip

In terms of performance, POCO X3 Pro possesses a powerful, advanced performance with Snapdragon 860 chip manufactured on 7 nm process for the ability to handle tasks quickly and smoothly.

Accompanying that is the 8 GB RAM capacity to meet the ability to multi-task with heavy applications in an outstanding way.

LiquidCool 1.0 Plus cooling technology will not let you down, helping to spread heat in all directions so you don't stop playing games at maximum configuration.

The machine has 256 GB internal memory capacity UFS 3.1 standard for large storage space combined with high read and write speeds to help POCO X3 Pro load games and applications is now done faster.

System of 4 sharp 48 MP cameras

The POCO X3 Pro phone is equipped with a quad rear camera system with a main sensor with a resolution of up to 48 MP. The remaining lenses are 8 MP wide-angle camera, 2 MP macro camera and 2 MP rear camera that supports portrait photography.

The high-resolution camera cluster and many photography features such as autofocus, exposure, ... easily produce high-quality movies and photos, clear and true to every detail.

Battery capacity 5160 mAh, support fast charging 33 W

POCO X3 Pro with a 5160 mAh battery helps the device to operate continuously for a long time.

In particular, the device is also equipped with 33W fast charging technology to quickly replenish energy, ensuring you are always ready for every journey.

The device is still equipped with fingerprint security technology integrated in the power button, both increasing the aesthetics of the design and making unlocking faster and more convenient.
