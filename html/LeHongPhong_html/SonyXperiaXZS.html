The design of the Sony Xperia XZs exudes luxury

The Xperia XZs inherits the design of the Xperia XZ with a monolithic metal frame, but the difference is that the back of the device is more matte, making it less prone to sweat and fingerprints.

The side edges of the XZs are gently curved for a comfortable grip, while the top and bottom are still flattened to create a sturdy, masculine and luxurious look.

Sony Xperia XZs display is sharp

Similar to Xperia XZ, Xperia XZs owns a 5.2-inch Full HD screen, using IPS LCD panels for very beautiful display images, high contrast, and faithfully reproduced colors.

Sony Xperia XZs has a waterproof feature

With Xperia XZs, users are completely confident when listening to the phone in the rain or taking pictures in the pool because the device is dustproof - waterproof according to IP68 standard, the highest standard of protection.

Sony Xperia XZs performance is very strong

Not equipped with Snapdragon 835 chip like brother Xperia XZ Premium, but with Snapdragon 820 chip, the performance of Xperia XZs is still very good.

Combined with 4GB RAM, the device runs fast, smooth, almost without reloading applications when multitasking.

Xperia XZs integrated fingerprint sensor and fast charging technology

With a fingerprint sensor integrated into the power button located on the right side, Xperia XZs helps users unlock the device quickly. At the same time, this is also a measure to improve the security of the device.

In addition, Xperia XZs also supports fast charging according to Qualcomm's Quick Charge 3.0 standard, allowing to charge up to 80% battery in just 30 minutes.

Sony Xperia XZs comes pre-installed with Android 7.0

Sony smartphones are pre-installed with Android 7.0 Nougat operating system when launched, this is the Android version introduced by Google in August 2016 with many new smart features and the ability to save memory, good power management. more, helping applications operate more smoothly and efficiently.

Camera Sony Xperia XZs inherits the modern technology of XZ Premium

Genuine Sony Xperia XZs retains the camera highlights from Xperia XZ Premium. Specifically, the main camera of the device has a resolution of 19MP, a large aperture (f / 2.0), integrated with Motion Eye technology that allows motion prediction to take 4 photos 2 seconds before the time of shooting.

At the same time, along with the Xperia XZ Premium, it is also the world's first device with a memory stacked sensor that enables slow-motion video recording at 960fps. Meanwhile, the front camera also has a resolution of up to 13MP, meeting the needs of selfie photography well.

Possessing a premium design, powerful performance, built-in waterproofing and many modern camera technologies, the genuine Sony Xperia XZs is a smartphone worth considering.
