Familiar design

The Sony Xperia XZ1 phone still carries the familiar design language of Sony in recent years with square, masculine edges.

The device is equipped with 5.2-inch TRILUMINOSTM screen technology with Full HD resolution to help display good images, true colors and significantly enhanced battery life thanks to the not-too-big screen.

In addition, Sony also equips the machine with HDR screen technology that provides superior display capabilities compared to screens of the same resolution.

Top configuration

The Sony Xperia XZ1 is likely to own a high-end 64-bit octa-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 835 chip, 4 GB of RAM and 64 GB of internal memory.

Powerful configuration helps Xepria XZ1 handle multitasking tasks well, playing games with high graphics.

In addition, the device also has an expansion slot with a MicroSD card that supports up to 256 GB, 4G LTE Cat 16 connection with lightning speed, the new operating system Android 8.0 stable, secure and many utilities.

The battery capacity is not too large with 2,700 mAh, but thanks to the efficiency optimization, it still gives you quite a comfortable use time for about 1 day.

Quality camera

The XZ1 will continue to build on the strengths of the previous XZs with the ability to record super slow motion video at up to 960 frames per second giving users unique footage to share with friends.

The main camera will remain the same 19 MP resolution, equipped with a three-dimensional image sensor and especially a 5-axis anti-shake system that provides better image quality when shooting.

The front camera also has a resolution of up to 13 MP, supports autofocus and has a built-in beauty mode that delivers quality selfies.
