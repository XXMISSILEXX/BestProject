Trendy edge-to-edge mole screen

The mid-range phone Xiaomi Redmi Note 9 possesses a soft curved design at the edges, for a more comfortable and easy-to-grip experience. The back is still highlighted and easily recognizable from afar with the outstanding camera cluster, typical of this year's Redmi Note series.

The front of the Note 9 is a 6.53-inch wide screen, with a small "mole" groove in the left corner of the screen. Xiaomi calls this screen DotDisplay with high-grade Corning Gorilla Glass 5 protection.

The device is also certified by TÜV Rheinland, which guarantees blue light protection to protect the eyes. IPS panels with Full HD + resolution provide an eye-catching, clear viewing and gaming experience in most lighting conditions.

The fingerprint sensor on the Redmi Note 9 is not integrated on the side like its brothers Note 9S, Note 9 Pro but has been moved to the back, for a more familiar usage experience.

In addition, the device also has a face security feature that makes unlocking the device faster and more accurate.

Breakthrough performance with Helio G85

Xiaomi Redmi Note 9 is the first smartphone launched with the Helio G85 gaming chip, with 8 cores of 2.0 GHz. The RAM and ROM memory of the device is 4 GB and 128 GB, respectively.

See also: MediaTek officially announced the Helio G85 chip, but it lacks the technology it should have at this time

The configuration of the device is at a good level in the segment but still ensures that the phone can play good and stable games with popular games like Lien Quan. Free, Fire. Daily tasks are less laggy, for smooth performance.

Compared with the previous generation Redmi Note 8, Xiaomi claims that this year's generation has 22% higher CPU power and 32% GPU power (Source: Mi.com).

Long-lasting battery, 18W fast charging

Comfortably experience with a huge battery of up to 5020 mAh, giving the ability to listen to calls for up to 33 hours continuously. Mixed use can reach more than 1 day with normal needs.

The device is also capable of 18W fast charging, which greatly shortens the charging time. Xiaomi also generously includes a fast charger inside the box.

48 MP super sharp photos, 4 versatile cameras

The photography ability is also an impressive highlight on the Redmi Note 9. The main camera of the device has a resolution of up to 48 MP for sharp and smooth images. The rest includes an 8 MP super wide-angle lens, a 2 MP font removal sensor and a 2 MP macro camera.

The main camera of the device not only performs excellently in bright enough conditions, but also performs well in low-light and complex lighting situations.

The remaining lenses fully meet the needs of fun and creative photography on smartphones.
