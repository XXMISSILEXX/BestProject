Elegant, sleek design

iPhone 8 Plus retains the finished design from the junior generation iPhone 7 Plus, besides the iPhone 8 Plus is rounded with soft edges to help increase the elegance and eye-catching. The device is equipped with a sturdy high-grade aluminum frame, and tempered glass on the front and back with the familiar apple logo.

The back is completely changed in design, with the use of glass material and the antenna is refined more neatly and subtly than previous iPhone versions.

The 5.5" LCD screen remains the same as the previous version, but the iPhone 8 Plus is improved in terms of large color coverage and True Tone technology, through ambient light sensing and automatically changing the phone screen brightness to help you less eye fatigue.

Experience the ultimate entertainment with terrible hardware configuration

Although it has been released for more than a year, the iPhone 8 Plus (64GB) is still highly appreciated in terms of performance, this device even outstrips the latest Android flagship models.

To have such good hardware power, Apple has equipped the iPhone 8 Plus with a 6-core A11 Bionic processor for excellent work performance.

Integrated GPU with 3 graphics processing cores commits to better experience heavy games or tasks that affect 3D graphics.

iOS plays an extremely important role in the performance story of the iPhone 8 Plus and many other iPhone models currently on the market.

The advantage of iOS is that it is a closed platform, through which Apple can actively optimize the application and hardware at its disposal and create a unity between the hardware and the app.

That's why iPhone 8 Plus, even with 3GB RAM, has better RAM/multitasking management than Android 4GB or even 6GB RAM models.

Powerful hardware and stable software, iPhone 8 Plus is committed to providing extremely good entertainment and entertainment tasks that hardly any other Android model in the same segment can do.

Beautiful screen, supports True tone and force touch

Although it should not be equipped with a 19:9 percent overflow design display, the iPhone 8 Plus with Retina display technology still offers very good image display capabilities.

5.5 inch size, FullHD resolution reaches about 401ppi, pixel density reproduces high detail and sharpness.

Supporting True tone feature allows the screen to change color according to the surrounding environment, thereby protecting the user's eyes when looking at the phone screen for a long time.

Force touch is a very interesting feature that Apple has made, in iPhone 8 Plus force touch (3D Touch) also actively supports relaxation. For example, in the PUBG game, users can use force sensing to replace the "shoot" key, which is very convenient.

Dual camera to remove professional fonts

The dual camera system on the back includes a 12MP + 12MP sensor cluster linked with a good application platform for accurate depth measurement and subject distance, professional font removal effects.

Regarding the selfie camera, although the image sensor is only at 7MP, the algorithm to solve the selfie image is always highly detailed, so users can freely apply beauty filters from the software. 3rd party very easily without worrying about the selfie photo losing detail or losing quality.

Good battery life

Owning a 2,691 mAh battery, this is a not too large battery compared to most current mobile devices, but the iOS platform has helped the iPhone 8 Plus achieve extremely good usage time.

According to GSM, the iPhone 8 Plus is capable of up to 21 hours of talk time (3G) and 60 hours of continuous music playback, impressive numbers that Android devices with over 3,000 mAh batteries are unlikely to achieve.
