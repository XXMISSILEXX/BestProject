Design with many breakthroughs

The notch on the iPhone 13 has also changed compared to the previous generation, specifically this notch is made 20% smaller, while the thickness of the device remains the same. The biggest difference in design on this iPhone 2021 generation is the diagonal camera.

High brightness Super Retina XDR display screen

The iPhone 13 phone will use a high-quality OLED panel and will have a size of 6.1 inches, larger than the iPhone 13 mini (5.4 inches). With this panel with ProMotion technology to help iPhone 13 save battery to the maximum when in use. Users can also easily adjust the refresh rate to their liking.

In terms of visibility, delivering outstanding display quality with a high-resolution, high-brightness OLED screen. As a result, users can see clearly in a variety of lighting conditions, including outdoors.

Specifically, Super Retina XDR screen with high brightness up to 800 nits, and up to 1200 nits maximum with P3 wide color range, large contrast ratio. The outside of the screen is covered with an oleophobic layer to help prevent fingerprints. Thereby limiting the situation of dust and sweat during use.

Dual 12MP cameras with optical image stabilization

iPhone 13 has a big change in camera compared to the iPhone 12 Series. Specifically, the iPhone may be equipped with a new ultra-wide lens that helps the device show more details in low-light photos. Meanwhile, the wide-angle lens can capture more light, up to 47%, helping to improve the quality of photos and videos.

The camera cluster is equipped with optical image stabilization and a new sensor, so that the captured image is stable.

The number of lenses on the iPhone 13 remains the same compared to the iPhone 12, only the position of each lens is different. Both lenses still own 12MP resolution. In which, the wide-angle camera is equipped with an aperture of ƒ / 1.6 while the ultra-wide angle is ƒ / 2.4 with a 120-degree rotation angle.

With iP13, users can shoot professional movies with cinema mode. This camera cluster also supports users to take many different photos at the same time without lifting a finger. Especially with portrait mode that supports professional background blur to help the whole image focus on the subject that the user is aiming for.

In Smart HDR 4 shooting mode, the machine can recognize up to four different people in a frame. After that, we will proceed to optimize the light, contrast and eyebrow tone for each person, giving the best quality photo. If using at night to take low-light photos, then Deep Fusion mode activates and analyzes the exposure mode at each pixel.

Improved video recording

In terms of video recording capabilities, the iPhone 13 can support 4K video recording at three different frame rates. The device also supports optical image stabilization and 3x zoom capability. Thanks to that, it promises to bring professional filming capabilities.

iPhone 13 also supports many advanced customization tools with Dolby Vision technology and the ability to record HDR Video with 4K resolution. In particular, users can do everything on this phone from filming, editing to rendering videos smoothly.

Outstanding performance with Apple A15 chip

iPhone 13 Series will be equipped with Apple A15 Bionic chip, chip set manufactured on 5nm process. According to the manufacturer, the Apple A15 Bionic chip gives 50% faster CPU and 30% faster GPU than the competition.

Performance on the iPhone is undisputed. Still carrying a remarkable power thanks to the optimized Apple A15 chip, customized iOS operating system. iPhone 13 can also fight well with all the latest games at max graphics configuration, providing smooth gaming experiences.

New battery technology improves battery life

With a newly optimized processor, the iPhone 13 offers a longer battery life. Like every year, Apple does not disclose the exact battery capacity of its devices. However, according to the company, the battery life on the iPhone 13 will be significantly increased to about 2.5 hours compared to the previous generation.
