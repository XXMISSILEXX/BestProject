Young and trendy design

Galaxy A72 attracts users at first sight with its soft, sleek curves. The cluster of 4 rear cameras is housed in the familiar rectangular module with LED flash, the color of the module and the back is designed with the same color to help the overall become more synchronized and harmonious.

Galaxy A72 owns many youthful colors for users to choose from: Purple, white, blue and black. This color set of Galaxy A72 emphasizes elegance, grace and is equally elegant and luxurious.

Besides the slender shape for a comfortable grip, the Galaxy A72 has extremely durable resistance, the device is dust and water resistant to IP67 standard, can withstand water at a depth of 1 meter up to a maximum of 1 meter. 30 minutes. Thanks to that, you can safely use the Galaxy A72 in all circumstances and environments.

Colorful entertainment on the ultimate screen

Galaxy A72 is equipped with a Super AMOLED panel for brilliant colors, high contrast and the ability to expand viewing angles, combined with a size of 6.7 inches, users can experience entertainment or work activities without interruption. most comfortable way.

Full HD+ resolution makes your visual experience more beautiful. Maximum brightness supports up to 800 nits, 90 Hz refresh rate for smoother, more fluid movements and screen operations.
