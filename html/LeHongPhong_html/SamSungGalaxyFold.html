Design 2 screens, flexible screen

Samsung Galaxy Fold not only owns a flexible screen, but also has a separate screen, so that it can be used independently when folded.

When recent smartphones are increasingly similar in design, the introduction of Samsung Galaxy Fold really creates a new breeze in the market.

You will have a screen with a size of 7.3 inches that you can use very comfortably as a tablet.

Feel free to use multiple apps at the same time

Owning a device with a large screen, users will have a more comfortable experience of watching movies or playing games.

Besides, you can also take advantage of this screen to use multiple applications at the same time quite easily.

Performance at the top of the table

The new Samsung smartphone equipped with the most powerful configuration of the Android world in 2019 is the powerful Snapdragon 855 chipset with up to 12 GB RAM.

The internal memory of the device also has a huge capacity of up to 512 GB, which is comfortable for you to install games, applications as well as store personal data.

Of course, the device will be pre-installed with Android 9.0 (Pie) operating system to bring efficiency optimization, smooth experience.

On the Galaxy Fold, apps like WhatsApp, Microsoft Office, and YouTube are all optimized for the new screen type.

There are up to 6 cameras on the device

Samsung Galaxy Fold owns a set of 3 main cameras on the back with the ability to take portraits as well as support wide-angle lenses for landscape shots.

The front camera is a dual camera cluster and the device also has an additional camera located on the secondary screen so you can still take selfies even when the device is folded.

Photos taken from the camera of the Samsung Galaxy Fold phone have good quality, true colors and many unique beauty and font removal effects.

Large battery capacity

The device has 2 batteries that are separated at the hinge of the phone for a total capacity of 4,380 mAh, this buffalo battery phone promises to meet all day needs.

Of course, to fully charge the battery with a fairly large capacity, Samsung also equips the device with fast charging capability to help you shorten the time to fully charge the device.
