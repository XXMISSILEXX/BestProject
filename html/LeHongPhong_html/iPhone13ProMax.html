Elegant flat edge design, colorful highlights

The iPhone 12 series is applied by Apple with the same design language as last year's iPhone 12 with luxurious flat edges and four slightly curved corners, this can be considered a nostalgic design from the iPhone line. 5 earlier. Therefore, the iPhone 13 Pro Max in particular, as well as the iPhone 13 series in general, will also follow this design language.

The big change on the 13 Pro Max may be the protrusion of the camera cluster. Meanwhile, the front of the device is covered with a layer of Ceramic Shield, a material that is harder than any smartphone glass currently on the market, which effectively protects the iPhone screen.

In terms of materials, iPhone 13 Pro Max still applies the same stainless steel material as the previous generation. This surface is also covered with a layer of frosted glass to help limit dust and fingerprints. In addition, the phone will also ensure IP68 dust / water resistance.

In terms of color, iPhone 13 Pro Max version will class="img-all-detail" be launched with many different color options. Including some outstanding colors such as: gold, silver, blue, black, ...

Screen with smaller ears, refresh rate 120 Hz

One of the factors that makes the iPhone 13 Pro Max desirable is the reduced "rabbit ears" notch design. In addition to the 6.7-inch screen size with Super Retina XDR OLED panel, the device will have a narrowed notch design, helping to increase the display ratio on the phone screen. Of course, important sensors like TrueDepth, Face ID or selfie camera will all stay in place.

Many sources say that the iPhone 13 Pro Max will greatly enhance the visual experience through ProMotion - a feature that helps push the screen refresh rate to 120 Hz. This is also the first iPhone with a high refresh rate of up to 120Hz, making moving images smoother.

In addition, other additional features such as Dolby Vision, True-tone or HDR10 standard wide color gamut will still be integrated into the iPhone 13 Pro Max.

Upgraded camera, quality photography, professional video recording

Like every other new iPhone, the camera is always the factor that users care about the most, and the iPhone 13 Pro Max will not disappoint users. Similar to body language, camera design language usually doesn't happen in a short time. That's why this phone will still have a 3-lens camera set alternately in a square cluster, located in the upper left corner of the back of the device.

The first big camera change is in the sensor. The device is equipped with a vastly improved super wide-angle camera with f/1.8 aperture lens and 6P lens. The current ultra-wide camera on the iPhone 12 Pro Max is at f/2.4 aperture and 5P lens, so the above upgrade will improve low-light photos a lot.

The LiDAR sensor will still be present on the iPhone 13 Pro Max. However, Apple is expected to bring this sensor to the entire iPhone 13 family and not just for the Pro devices. This is supposed to provide the best augmented reality (AR) experience for all users.

The selfie camera is unchanged from its predecessor. Maybe the iPhone 13 Pro Max will take a slightly wider-angle selfie, but the rest of the selfie camera remains the same. And features like slow-mo selfie, memoji and animoji will also be present as many rumors suggest.

Upgraded processor, improved battery capacity

Like many new iPhone generations, the processor is expected to be upgraded to a level with significantly improved performance and the ability to support 5G networks. In this case, the iPhone 13 Pro Max will carry the Apple A15 Bionic chip with various upgrades, both in terms of processing performance and high-speed internet connection.

According to the manufacturer, this new generation A15 chip offers up to 50% faster graphics performance than other smartphone devices. In addition, 5G connectivity is also significantly improved, along with a smart data mode that reduces speed when not needed to save power.

iPhone 13 Pro Max is also equipped with a larger capacity battery, for an increased use time of about 2.5 hours compared to the previous generation. The device will also be equipped with fast charging technology and fast wireless charging.
