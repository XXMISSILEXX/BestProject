Shedding in a new, attractive shape

The Sony Xperia XZ3 has a user-experience-oriented design with a soft, curved body that feels more solid and hugs the hand.

The body of the phone is machined from 7000-series aluminum alloy to make the device look more sturdy and stronger, as well as limit distortion during strong impacts.

The front and back are 2 layers of high-grade tempered glass rounded into the body with an extremely sophisticated level of finishing to create a uniform block.

The machine is adorned with 4 attractive colors to help you easily choose a suitable color for yourself.

In general, the design of this Sony phone is not too different from the elder XZ2, but just a little change makes the device attractive.

Super sharp screen up to 2K+ resolution

Sony is always at the forefront of display quality, so it is obvious that the Xperia XZ3 is considered a representative representative with a screen size of up to 6 inches with 2K + resolution.

Not only that, the machine is also integrated with HDR OLED image technology to give you a better experience every time you watch high-quality movies.

Top performance with Snapdragon 845

The power of Xperia XZ3 equipped by Sony is Snapdragon 845 chip with strong ability to handle today's top heavy tasks.

Unfortunately, the device has only 4 GB of RAM, which is quite confusing for a flagship flagship of the company.

But in return, the device will be pre-installed with Android 9.0 with a new interface and many added features, promising to make your experience a lot more interesting.

Camera is slightly upgraded

Although it does not own a dual camera cluster according to the current trend, the image quality on the Xperia XZ3 is still among the first in the smartphone village today.

In terms of parameters like its elder brother, the Xperia XZ3 is still equipped with a 19 MP sensor that supports 5-axis optical stabilization and the ability to record slow-motion videos at 960fps.

The biggest change is that the selfie camera cluster has now been upgraded by Sony to a resolution of 13 MP with a large aperture of f / 1.9 to help capture more light when shooting in low light conditions.

Many high-end equipment is still maintained

First of all, the water and dust resistance standard that the Xperia XZ3 is equipped with makes the device safer in force majeure cases.

Besides, there is a USB Type C port that supports Quick Charge 4+ fast charging technology for convenient charging and data transfer.

Moreover, the dual speaker system of the device is now fine-tuned for good sound quality and is about 20% louder than its elder brother.

Not stopping there, vibration technology and continuous haptic feedback to the music will help you feel excited when playing games.

In addition, the battery of the device now has a higher capacity with 3,330 mAh, although not too terrible, but that is enough for you to feel satisfied every time you use it.
