iPhone 12 Pro Max: Raise the level of use

Every year, towards the end of August and near the beginning of September, all new exciting information about the new iPhone appears. Apple this year again released a new iPhone with the new name iPhone 12 Pro Max, this is a new and most powerful phone line of the Apple family this year. Please refer to some more product descriptions below so you can make a purchasing decision.

Screen 6.7 inches Super Retina XDR

This year, the screen technology on the 12 Pro Max is also renewed and better equipped with the same size up to 6.7 inches, larger than the iPhone 12. With OLED screen technology for image visibility up to 2778 x 1284 pixels. Besides, this screen also gives the highest maximum brightness of up to 800 nits, always ensuring you a high brightness and easy to see in the sun.

Another innovation point on the screen of this year's Apple iPhone 12 phone is that they are designed with a square frame, stainless steel border to bring a luxurious beauty to the phone. The device is also equipped with many special color versions for users to choose from.

6GB RAM for comfortable multitasking, large internal memory

Regarding internal hardware, the iPhone 12 Pro Max has a stick of RAM up to 6GB. This shows that Apple is listening to users more and more when equipped with a larger RAM capacity to improve multitasking. The large ram bar helps you experience games and multitasking more smoothly.

This year, 12 Pro Max will also have three different versions of internal memory, with the smallest internal memory starting from 128GB, 256GB and the highest will be 512GB. A phone that has an internal memory as large as a laptop is what Apple wants to give users to be able to share some memory with other devices.

Apple A14 SoC 5nm chip, powerful 6GB RAM

iPhone 12 Pro Max not only has the above improvements, but they also have an improvement that is considered the core source and the heart to operate the super phone 2020, which is the 5nm Apple A14 SoC chip. This equipment gives the phone a formidable power over its competitors in terms of performance and battery performance.

The device is also equipped with wifi and mobile network connectivity standards to help you improve network performance, and they also help ensure signal transmission lines are always guaranteed to not drop the connection and increase image display quality Online.

Cluster of 3 rear cameras with 12MP resolution

It can be said that the camera is also a new step forward on the iPhone 12 Pro Max when they have a set of 3 cameras with a common resolution of 12MP. However, they have a large aperture and a larger pixel density on a panel, so they give more detailed images, better light capture. In addition, combined with OIS optical stabilization, the device can also record good 4K movies.

The camera on the iPhone 12 Pro Max has depth scanning and ensures the image has a certain depth. Along with that, the main function of this lens is the ability to present 3D images when scanning them into a certain room. Help for construction work as well as image format before construction.

12MP front camera supports FaceiD opening and IP68 waterproof technology

The 12MP front camera also has the same aperture and pixel size in the same panel as the camera. This makes using them for better and more realistic selfies. Along with that a feature that Apple has always kept them from the iPhone X to now is the ability to scan 3D FaceiD faces.

Waterproof technology is indispensable on high-end phones and especially for iPhone 12 Pro Max. They are equipped with the best waterproof and dustproof technology on smartphones today, which is IP68 standard. Helping you always have more peace of mind when using filming underwater or in harsh environments.

Instant battery for up to all day use with fast charging technology

An integrated battery with large capacity on iPhone 12 Pro Max helps your phone to operate more smoothly and use it for a longer time. Specifically, the device gives up to 80 hours of music playback or 12 hours of online video playback.

Charging technology is on the 12 Pro Max which is fast wireless charging technology up to 15W. This can help you avoid having to wait for your phone to finish charging when it can replenish 50% of its capacity in just 30 minutes.
