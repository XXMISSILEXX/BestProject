Review iPhone 11 - Upgraded camera, youthful colors

Apple always pleases iFan followers with iPhone lines in each price segment. In particular, the iPhone 11 version has just been launched but has dominated the smartphone market worldwide with affordable prices. Let's find out more details through the following article to decide whether to buy or not!

Luxurious design, inheriting iPhone XR

The rear camera cluster has been rearranged to resemble the Huawei Mate 20 Pro. The iPhone 11 screen has a wide and long notch like its predecessors and retains the same design as the iPhone XR. Notch integrates Face ID, audio speaker and selfie camera.

IPS LCD panels have no improvement in brightness and contrast. The panel is covered by high-quality Gorilla Glass, the edge edge has a rather small curved angle. The phone is light in weight, easy to hold at only 194 grams. The detailed dimensions are 5.94H x 2.98W x 0.33 D (inches) respectively.

6.1 inch screen, HD Retina display technology

Apple applies liquid Retina HD screen technology with a 6.1-inch full-screen LCD multi-touch screen with 326 ppi for iPhone 11. The screen has an actual resolution of 828x1792 pixels, just enough. In addition, True Wireless technology is integrated while the HDR video contrast ratio standard is only displayed on OLED screens.

The screen lag is almost zero, the screen refresh rate is at 60 Hz. Color accuracy reaches the maximum, the average DeltaE score is 1.1, the maximum deviation is 2.4 RGB. Even when the brightness drops to 2.3 nits, this accuracy remains the same.

Powerful A13 chipset, outstanding performance

iPhone 11 uses Apple A13 Bionic Chipset with 4GB Ram. This chip is considered to be energy efficient and runs faster than the A12 generation. With 6-core 2x2.65 GHz Lightning and thunder 4x1.8 GHz CPU. The 3rd generation Neural Engine has 8 cores in charge of image processing features such as Night Mode and Deep Fusion.

iOS 13 operating system is used for iPhone 11, if you want to install to higher versions, you need to update the system. The FaceID feature is used by the application to increase the security of the phone. The 3D Touch feature has been replaced by Haptic Touch. Later versions may apply Haptic Touch.

Impressive rear camera, optical anti-shake technology

iPhone 11 has a total of 2 rear cameras: 2 rear cameras and 1 flash. The main camera has a resolution of 12 MP with OIS optical image stabilization technology, a 12 MP wide-angle camera.

Main camera with 28mm focal length, f/1.8 aperture, Dual Pixel AF, optical stabilization and Night Mode available on camera. 13mm wide-angle camera without focus, no optical stabilization.

Low noise image quality, sharp details with extremely impressive dynamic color range. Because the colors are true and clear, it is inevitable that they will fade and lack vitality. The wide-angle camera helps to make the image more detailed, and the color, contrast and dynamic resolution are contrasted with the main camera.

When shooting in low-light environments, at night, Night Mode is the savior at this time. The image quality is detailed, clear, clear enough, the impression is not blurred. In the case of portraiture, different lighting effects expand your choice. With the 13th processor chip, separating the subject and the background becomes simpler than ever.

Realistic selfie camera with wide color range

iPhone 11 selfie camera has a resolution of 12MP, 22mm focal length, f/2.2. The smart HDR feature makes photos much more detailed, true, and sharper with a wide color range. With the normal need for "selfie", you were able to capture all the happy moments with friends and relatives.

Large battery capacity, Qi wireless charging compatible

Compared to iPhone XR, iPhone 11 has a battery capacity of up to 3110 mAh, which is enough for you to perform common tasks for a long time. You can watch videos comfortably for up to 17 hours, music playback up to 65 hours, online video playback up to 10 hours.

The phone supports Qi-compatible wireless charging, within 30 minutes you can charge about 50% of the battery with a 20 W charger or more. Alternatively, you can also charge via USB to a computer system or adapter.

TrueDepth recognition technology, IP68 . waterproof

iPhone 11 owns TrueDepth facial recognition technology. In addition, when paying at different stores, applications or on a web browser, you can completely pay with Face ID.

In addition, in terms of water and dust resistance, the smartphone from Apple has an IP68 rating according to IEC 60529, which means it's water resistant to a maximum depth of 2 meters for 30 minutes.
