Similar design to iPhone 12 Pro, innovative camera cluster

The iPhone 13 Pro does not have many design changes, still possessing the same design as the iPhone 12 Pro with squarer edges and a stainless steel frame. In terms of size, the iPhone 13 Pro will be about 0.2mm thicker than the iPhone 12 Pro with dimensions of 146.7 x 71.5 x 7.6 mm.

The iPhone 13 Pro camera cluster will also have an innovation, still a camera cluster on a square module, but the protrusion will be thicker with the size of the camera cluster increasing to 29 x 29 mm.

Besides the familiar colors, the iPhone 13 Pro also owns more trendy new colors including color versions: Sierra Blue, Silver, Gold and Graphite. And maybe in the future, Apple will add new colors to the collection.

6.1 inch screen, LTPO OLED screen and 120Hz refresh rate

The iPhone 13 Pro screen has a smaller notch than the iPhone 12 Pro, so the display space will also be more optimal. The ultra-thin screen border helps increase the viewing angle for users and with the 6.1-inch size, the smartphone is still compact enough for you to easily pocket. The front of the device is also equipped with a sturdy ceramic shield, protecting the phone screen.

Equipped with a Super Retina XDR OLED display with 120Hz refresh rate, so the screen can refresh from 10 to 120 times per second. Therefore, iP 13 Pro promises to bring you better, smoother visual experiences, comfortably enjoy the game space, watch movies, entertain or work better with smartphones.

OLED screen technology helps display content more vividly, even in bright sunlight. This screen panel also possesses great contrast with deep blacks, bright whites.

Camera with 3 improved lenses, better night photography

Like iPhone 12 Pro, iPhone 13 Pro also owns a triple camera system including main camera, super wide-angle camera along with telephoto camera and LiDAR sensor with useful photography features such as Night Mode, HDR, Panorama mode. , Timeslape,...

In particular, the super wide-angle lens supports focusing at a distance of 2cm, so that users can take artistic photos of small details such as dew drops, caterpillar fluff,... In addition to the ability to Taking photos, iPhone 13 Pro also supports macro video recording with slow motion or fast forward at will.

The device is also improved in night shooting capabilities. With the LiDAR scanner, the device supports quality night mode portraits. Meanwhile, 2.2 times more light is captured in the wide-angle camera and 92% in the ultra-wide lens, resulting in clear night shots. In addition, the telephoto lens with a focal length of 77 mm and 3x optical zoom supports well in taking classic portraits or taking photos and videos from a distance.

Similar to the iPhone 13, the iPhone 13 Pro is also equipped with a cinematic recording mode. The mode allows editing effects and depth when shooting. There are also many other smart photography technologies on this new 2021 iPhone model such as Smart HDR 4, Deep Fusion.

More powerful configuration when equipped with A15 Bionic chip, memory up to 1TB

With the new powerful chip and upgraded performance, the A15 Bionic, the iPhone 13 Pro will have more power. This chip also better supports screen refresh rate as well as improves power consumption to save battery life for smartphones.

iPhone 13 Pro is also integrated with a large RAM capacity for powerful multitasking and memory capacity of up to 1TB, so for users who need to use more data, you can comfortably store it with iPhone 13 Pro.

Larger battery, supports fast wireless charging

Because of its thicker size, Apple equips the iPhone 13 Pro with a larger battery, helping to provide enough power for iPhone technologies while ensuring the duration for users. Specifically, iPhone 12 Pro for up to 1.5 hours of use compared to the previous generation.
