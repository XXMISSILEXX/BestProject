The 6.1-inch screen makes the iPhone XR perfect for those who like to own a large screen

At 6.1 inches, the iPhone XR has a larger screen than the XS but is smaller than the Max models. According to many reviews, although the Liquid Retina HD screen technology on the iPhone XR is not as good as the OLED of the X, XS or 11 Pro series, most users will not be able to distinguish the quality of images and videos when displayed on the screen. these two screens.

The iPhone XR supports a wide color gamut and handy display features like True Tone that filters out blue light for better nighttime viewing. However, this device does not support HDR.

iPhone XR gets Face ID, Animoji, and Memoji features

Face ID is a security feature that was introduced with the iPhone X in 2017. Also known as "face recognition technology" (using your face to unlock the phone) is the most secure and modern available today. . Even so, users are always debating its convenience compared to Touch ID.

Along with securely unlocking your phone with your face, the True Depth camera is also the only way to access Apple's new Memoji and Animoji. This is a fun way to interact with friends and family whether it's through iMessage or FaceTime.

A12 Bionic chip delivers fast and efficient performance on iPhone XR

Apple's A12 Bionic chip was released in 2018, but it's been working great so far. According to the benchmarks, the A12 delivers near-desktop CPU performance, which means it supports running apps quickly and incredibly smoothly.

The iPhone XR's camera is one of the best for photos and videos

Although it still uses a 12MP main camera, which looks similar to the camera on the previous generation iPhone 8 Plus, the technology and camera features on the iPhone XR are much different.

First of all, the daylight photos are of unsurpassed quality. This is largely thanks to Apple's Smart HDR technology, which provides superior dynamic range in well-lit shots and eliminates the long shooting times found in previous-generation Auto HDR. Apple does this by snapping photos the moment the camera app is opened, combining them into a single image at the moment the shutter button is pressed - no need to wait.

When combined with the improved Portrait mode with Depth Control, the iPhone XR camera is a huge leap in quality over previous-generation iPhones even though it only has a single camera lens on the back. But it doesn't just stop at the pictures…

iPhone XR also offers improved 4K 60fps video recording with stereo sound - making videos shot with this phone incredibly crisp and vivid.

IP67 water resistance is good for accident prevention

Water resistance is an important feature in most newer phones these days. With the iPhone XR, it comes with IP67 rating for water resistance. Although a bit low, it still allows the phone to survive in a depth of 1 meter of water for 30 minutes.
