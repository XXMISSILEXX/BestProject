Compact design, square frame

In terms of external design, the iPhone 13 Mini will not be too different from its predecessor. The device is still equipped with a small design, fits in the palm of the hand. The bezel on this iPhone version is also still squarely finished with a Ceramic Shield glass front and an aluminum back.

The biggest difference in the design of this version is the camera cluster. Instead of being placed vertically like the iPhone 12 mini, the camera on the iPhone 13 mini will be diagonally designed. The notch in front of the screen is also more compact.

Super Retina XDR . Display

The first upgrade point on the iPhone 13 Mini is the screen frequency. This new 2021 iPhone phone is upgraded to a Super Retina XDR screen so that touches and swipes take place smoothly.

In terms of visibility, iPhone 13 Mini is equipped with a screen with high resolution and brightness. Thanks to that, iPhone 13 Mini will bring impressive photos

Upgraded performance with Apple A15 Bionic chip

Performance is the upgrade point on this iPhone model. The phone will be equipped with the Apple Apple A15 chip that is machined on an advanced process. Thanks to this new generation chip, the iPhone 13 mini will have 20% stronger performance than the iPhone 12 mini generation. With this configuration, users can comfortably experience surfing the web, listening to music, playing games without experiencing lag or lag.

Besides, there is RAM capacity and 128GB internal memory in the standard version.

Improved battery capacity, fast charging support

Compared to the 2,227 mAh capacity of the iPhone 12 Mini, the battery on the iPhone 13 mini is upgraded. Combined with the Apple A15 energy-saving chip, the iPhone 13 mini provides stable usage time.

In addition, the device will also still be equipped with convenient fast wireless charging and fast charging technology for users.

Upgraded sensor camera

The iPhone 13 mini phone is upgraded in terms of camera to bring impressive photography quality. Specifically, the device will be equipped with an enhanced sensor, which only appears on the iPhone 12 pro Max. The back of the device is still equipped with a dual camera but there are changes in the design. The front is still a single camera located in the notch.
