iPhone 7 Plus design is old but still beautiful

iPhone 7 Plus has a size commensurate with other smartphones on the market and is made with luxurious metal materials. Although the iPhone 7 Plus can be bent due to strong impact, this metal material makes the iPhone 7 Plus look luxurious. The machine has a roughened back to help limit fingerprints compared to a device with a glossy back, in return, the half-bite apple logo is glossy and attractive.

In addition to the rough style, the point that attracts attention on the back of the iPhone 7 Plus is the rear camera cluster. Despite the age, the dual camera cluster of the iPhone 7 Plus still delivers beautiful photo quality. I will explain more in the later part of the article.

The feeling of holding the iPhone 7 Plus is very pleasant thanks to the soft curved back on the sides. With the latest generation of iPhones, the iPhone 12 Series, the design has become more square, but I still appreciate the soft style of the iPhone 7 Plus and its comfortable grip.

Meanwhile, on the front, iPhone 7 Plus owns a 5.5-inch screen with two thick top and bottom edges. Personally, I find this style gradually fades over time as the bezel-less screen design becomes more and more popular. But the thick border makes it easier to use and manipulate, and prevents accidental touches.

iPhone 7 Plus screen resolution is Full HD and the device is integrated with IPS LCD panel for high brightness. Forget it, the iPhone 7 Plus also has a fingerprint sensor built into the Home button that has become the identification point of Apple's iPhone line. But when your hands are sweaty, unlocking will be difficult.

Apple A10 on iPhone 7 Plus still delivers smooth performance

With the above configuration level, iPhone 7 Plus still delivers smooth performance with popular games on the market today. I fought Lien Quan Mobile right on iPhone 7 Plus and found that the FPS fluctuated quite stable from 58-61 FPS. Although it sometimes drops to 56 FPS, this situation does not happen much.
