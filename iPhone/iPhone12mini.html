The iPhone 12 Mini version is one of Apple's super product versions, in this model the edge of the device is no longer designed with curved edges, but instead has an extremely strong beveled edge. strong and personal.

The familiar notch-shaped screen, with a more streamlined notch, gives the impression of a larger screen even though the iPhone 12 Mini has a screen size of only 5.4 inches.

The 5.4-inch screen is an advantage because the device is quite compact, can be easily placed in the pocket, pants than 6.1 inches on the iPhone 12 Pro or 6.7 inches on the 12 Pro Max.

The screen of the iPhone 12 Mini has a resolution of 2340x1080, every detail and movement on the screen is clear, bright and does not experience blurring or jerking.

The highlight of this phone line is in the camera, the camera of the iPhone 12 Mini phone with dual 12MP cameras so that the images will be recorded clearly, without breaking the image when zoomed in.
