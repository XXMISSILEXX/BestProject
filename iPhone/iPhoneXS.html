Design of iPhone XS

Still a metal frame design with 2 glass sides. Apple iPhone XS brings weight to the user with a comfortable grip. With 2.5D curved front and back glass, hugging the frame.

And the design is small, slim, convenient to put in the pocket or take out. This is one of the premium elements that every Apple product is aiming for.

If before with products like iPhone XR or iPhone 8 plus, the edge of it was made of aluminum. Then with the iPhone XS product line, the edge is designed with stainless steel.

For better bearing capacity than aluminum rim, thereby protecting your phone better. And the steel border of the XS series is better than the iPhone X because this line is easy to scratch if used for a long time.

iPhone XS screen

Compared to the elder iPhone X, the iPhone XS is more focused on visibility. When equipped with a series of high-end technologies such as: Owning a 5.8-inch rabbit-ear OLED screen with 2k resolution. Bringing you a great experience when watching movies or surfing the web with wide color gamut and extremely high detail.

Moreover, the screen of the device also supports HDR10 technology with a refresh rate of 120 Hz. Helps vivid images also move smoother. You can use it indoors or outdoors.

In addition, a plus point for the iPhone XS is that it retains the 3D Touch feature. Bring convenience to users

Configuration of iphone XS

iPhone XS is equipped with the new Apple A12 Bionic processor chip, which Apple said will give the iPhone generation superior processing capabilities.

iPhone XS Battery Review

Perhaps this is a limitation for the iPhone XS line with other products. When its capacity is only 2568mAh. This is not a very high number.

If you use it for listening, calling or texting and some simple tasks in a day, it's still good. But what if we play games or use the phone a lot. The battery is very quickly drained, about 4-5 hours is running out of battery.
