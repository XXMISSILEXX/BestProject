The design is familiar but still classy

It's been 3 years since the iPhone 6 was released, and Apple has not made any significant design changes on iPhone models.

The biggest change on the iPhone 8 compared to recent iPhone models is that Apple has returned to using glass back for its iPhones since the iPhone 4S until now.

This makes the iPhone 8 capable of convenient wireless charging, which Android smartphones have had for quite some time.

High quality and sharp display

The display of iPhones has always been highly appreciated by users and the iPhone 8 remains the same 4.7-inch size and HD resolution (1334 x 750 pixels) as on the iPhone 7.

Apple retrofitted the machine with True Tone technology, which existed before on iPads, giving the ability to automatically correct screen colors in many different environments.

There is a camera upgrade

The main camera has a resolution of 12 MP, an aperture of f / 1.8 and a lot of improvements in images, sharpness, speed or low light shooting.

Apple equips the device with the ability to take photos with different modes that provide professional and unique photos to show off to friends.

In which iPhone 8 also supports 4K - 60fps clip recording to help you have quality footage right with your phone.

A12 Bionic chip delivers fast and efficient performance on iPhone XR

The front camera of the device remains the same 7 MP resolution with f / 2.2 aperture and Retina Flash feature to help you take selfies even in low light conditions.

Configuration

iPhone 8 uses 6-core A11 Bionic chip like on iPhone X, the power coming from this processor is still nothing to complain when this is the chip with the best performance on smartphones of 2017.

The device is still only equipped with 2 GB of RAM, but with 256 GB of internal memory, you will comfortably save photos and videos.

Good security Touch ID

It is possible that the iPhone 8 will be the last iPhone generation that Apple also integrates Touch ID into the Home key of the device because now Face ID on the iPhone X is getting more attention and focus.

The fingerprint sensor on the device still gives very fast and accurate identification speed. In addition, the 1821 mAh battery ensures you to use it for about 1 day.
