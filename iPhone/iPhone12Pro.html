iPhone 12 Pro - Breakthrough in design, upgraded performance

Launched at the end of 2020, iPhone 12 series brings a breath of fresh air in the high-end smartphone segment. With a breakthrough innovation design compared to the previous generation and many significant performance upgrades on iPhone 12 Pro. This will be one of the phones worth choosing the most compared to other phones in the same price segment.

Breakthrough new design stainless steel frame, 6.1 inch Super Retina display

After a long time of launching new products, there are little changes in the external design. In 2020, iPhone 12 Pro brings a more breakthrough design style with a square CNC rounded edge like the direction of the brother iPhone 5.

The design material of the iPhone 12 Pro version is also made of stainless steel, providing a firmer and more sturdy grip. The colors on the phone in addition to the old base colors also have a more beautiful and subtle Navy Blue.

This year, the minimum internal memory on iPhone 12 Pro phones has been upgraded to 128Gb. Double the storage capacity compared to the most basic version like the previous generation iPhone Pro series.

Apple also supports many more capacities including 256Gb and 512Gb to users. With these capacity levels, users can comfortably store data, install heavy applications and games without being full of memory when using.

Triple rear camera + LiDAR sensor, super sharp front camera

Similar to the iPhone 12 Pro Max, the Pro version is also equipped with a trio of high-quality cameras with 12MP resolution, including a wide-angle main camera, an ultra-wide-angle camera and a telephoto lens. The device is also equipped with a LiDAR sensor lens for the ability to capture sharp and realistic images. The ability to shoot wide-angle and Zoom on the super product has also been significantly upgraded for better image quality and ability./p>

The front camera of the device is equipped with quite high resolution. For wider-angle selfie capabilities. With many shooting modes, images are reproduced and processed more intelligently with Apple's camera AI technology. You can comfortably use your smartphone for tasks such as taking selfies, recording with the front camera or making video calls through the app with ease.

High-capacity lithium-ion battery, 20W . fast charging support

It is a powerful phone with a large screen. So Apple has equipped the iPhone 12 Pro with a large battery capacity. With this capacity, users can comfortably use all day long without worrying about running out of battery. The device is also equipped with fast charging technology with large capacity for quick battery charging time.
