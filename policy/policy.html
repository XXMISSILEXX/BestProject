Warranty Policy

When buying products at YasuoShop.com, customers have the right to be completely assured of genuine products, officially warranted by the company and in addition have a free renewal policy up to 30 days at Yasuoshop

Conditions:

  • -Device: Like new, no scratches, no decals, decorations
  • -Used device: has the same product condition as when it was purchased
  • -Box: Like new, not dented, torn, broken, written, drawn, wrapped with tape, glue; have the Serial/IMEI on the box that matches the body.
  • -Accessories and gifts: Full, intact, not dented, scratched or damaged during use.
  • -Account: The device has been signed out of all accounts such as iCloud, Google Account, Mi Account
